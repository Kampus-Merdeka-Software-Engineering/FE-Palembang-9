Vocabulary Building: Learn New Words Every Day and Use Them in Sentences

Keep a Vocabulary Journal:

Maintain a notebook or digital document where you record new words you encounter. Include the word, its definition, a sample sentence, and any related information.

Read Widely:

Read a variety of materials, including books, newspapers, magazines, and online articles. Exposure to different contexts and genres will introduce you to diverse vocabulary.

Use Flashcards:

Create flashcards with the word on one side and the definition or a sentence using the word on the other side. Review these flashcards regularly to reinforce your memory.
