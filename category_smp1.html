Asking and Giving Attention

Asking for attention is an expression or phrase used to ask for other people's attention. The attention referred to can be in a broad sense, for example asking for attention when providing information, telling stories, speaking, and so on.
The use of the sentence asking for attention depends on the purpose. However, in general, asking for attention can be done in two ways, namely through command sentences and question sentences.

Example:
• Hello!
• Listen!
• Listen to me now!
• Attention, please!
• Pay attention, please!
• Look!
• Look at me!
